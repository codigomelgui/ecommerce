The Company

 


Electrónica MELGUI, S.L., is a spanish company specialized on deliverying electronic components in profesional sectors of industry and telecomunications. Our activity fields are the key sectors of profesional electronics, telecomunications, phone technology and some other industrial areas.

Electronica Melgui was founded in July, 1986. The strength, responsability and enthusiasm of their founders has brougth our company to reach a solid position in the electronic components market.

Our goal is to solve the problems of our customers, studying different solutions in order to offer the best option, always keeping our compromise with quality.

Quality Assurance

Since date of foundation, Electrónica Melgui S.L, has been dedicated to providing clients with reliable and quality electronic components. Our ISO9001:2008 certified management system ensures effective quality management of our entire operating system.
