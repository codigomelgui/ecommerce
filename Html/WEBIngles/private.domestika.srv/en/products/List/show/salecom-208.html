SALECOM

SALECOM ELECTRONICS. Salecom Electronics Co., Ltd. established in 1976, specializes in producing toggle switches, rocker switches, slide switches, pushbutton switches, dip switches and tact switches. As a switch professional manufacturer for over 30 years, Salecom has been supplying an extensive range of items to leading brands worldwide. The typical applications of our products are to telecommunications, instrumentation, medical equipment, telephone & cable modems, computer peripherals & network products, etc.
